Tsung-Han Lin (林宗翰)

Multimodal Learning, Vision–Language

I am a research assistant at National Taiwan University (NTU). I received a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics of Computation from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA).

My research interests lie in multimodal learning—exploring scalable architectures and frameworks that bridge vision and language. I’m especially curious about how different modalities can align to improve complex reasoning and produce more robust, context-aware outputs. Feel free to reach out if you share similar interests.
