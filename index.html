Tsung-Han (Hank) Lin

I am a research assistant at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) NLP Group. My research interests span vision and language, multimodal learning, and embodied AI, particularly in aligning multimodal models to perceive and reason about the real world.

Previously, I worked at National Taiwan University's Machine Discovery & Social Network Mining Lab with Prof. Shou-De Lin on vision-language models for physical design in collaboration with NVIDIA Design Automation Research Group. I completed my B.S. in Mathematics of Computation at UCLA.
